    • Making of My Portfolio

    Clean, intuitive navigation

    I made sure that navigation, articles, blogs, and overall information display was not overstimulating and were always present. you should be able to begin the navigation to any place in the site from any place. Navigation is both at the top and bottom of the pages and on the home page projects shown there is linked to the project page as well.

    Intentional color choices

    I prefer a simple site for my portfolio to aid in the clean style and to be able to have the use of color that is used that more effecient. Most of the color is in the pictures and thumbnails of the various post on the site and I like that as a contrast. Greys, whites, and black are the colors that I chose to work with and used them as layering and stucturing signifiers throughout the site.

    Harmony between affordances and signifiers

    I think from my hover effects, bolding, page structure, copy, and overall clean design there is good harmony in the signifier and affordances. There are limited things that I am trying to signify to a user so I think that the things that I can, I am doing a good job at balancing.

    Some consideration to accessibility

    Images having alt text, navigation through tabing, color limiting, and copy are all areas that I took into consideration as accessibility areas that I wanted to unsure were inclusive.

    Usability elements to help make content easier to digest

    I used lists, bolds, breaks, and sections with titles to help make content easier to digest. On the home page I have so ideally each section will only be on the screen one at a time. That was me thinking about a form of pagination in a vertical way.

    Evidence of evolution

    If you read some of the posts, you can see how I express my learning and things that I will use practically and some of the thing are even evidant in this site itself. When I talk about practicality, this portfolio is the practice.

    Proper attribution of others' work

    In posts that I am referencing information from a book or video, I link to it, or provice a citation at the beginning of post. I have embedded videos in some of the posts as well as direct links to articles that the post derived from.

    intentional integration of your values

    I like things to be clean, easy, and am straight forward person that hold himself to a certain standards. I think that these things are evident if you read the copy, navigate throught the site, and notice my use of color.
