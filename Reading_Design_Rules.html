    Word: Scanning

    Chapter 2 of Steve Krug's book dives into the notion that people typically don't read websites the way they read a book or a magazine. Instead, they scan. They look for keywords, short sentences, bulleted lists, and other visually distinct elements. This is crucial because, in design, it means that content should be organized and presented in a way that caters to this scanning behavior. For professionals, understanding this behavior is vital to creating web interfaces that users find intuitive and easy to navigate.
