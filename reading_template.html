    Authenticity. Growth. Giving Back.

    I consider myself a reflective individual, often seeking opportunities to look within. After reading Colin Breck's blog, I of course took the advice of the article and defined my core values. The three words that resonate deeply with me are: authenticity, growth, and giving back. These values not only represent who I am but also are instilled in everything I create and contribute to.

    It's important that our chosen values guide our decisions, both in life and in design. In the realm of design, there's a unique chance to convey feelings and values, much like a photograph or a movie scene. For instance, if you aim to project strength, consider adopting a bold design language complemented by assertive copy. Your visuals should scream confidence. Or if humor is your goal, weave in interactivity that invokes a chuckle, and ensure the copy is light-hearted.
